What is DisneyPlus.com/Begin?

DisneyPlus.com/Begin is a simple activation portal designed to help users set up Disney+ on various devices, such as smart TVs, tablets, or gaming consoles. It provides a fast way to link your Disney+ subscription to a compatible device and begin streaming immediately.

Requirements for Activating Disney+

Before you start, make sure you have the following:

  • A valid Disney+ subscription
  • An internet-connected device with the Disney+ app installed
  • Your Disney+ account credentials (email and password)

How to Activate Your Device Using DisneyPlus.com/Begin?

Follow these steps to activate Disney+ on your device:

  1. Open the Disney+ app on your device.
  2. Log in to your Disney+ account if prompted.
  3. Note the 8-digit code that appears on your screen.
  4. On a computer or smartphone, visit DisneyPlus.com/Begin.
  5. Enter the 8-digit code from your device and click "Continue."

After completing these steps, your device should be linked to your Disney+ account, allowing you to start streaming.
